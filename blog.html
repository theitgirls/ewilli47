All About Elizabeth S Friedman

Erica Williams / July 19th, 2022

Elizebeth Smith Friedman was an American expert cryptanalyst and author who deciphered enemy codes in both World Wars. She is also known for helping solve international smuggling cases during Prohibition. Her career in the workforce lasted from 1916 and carried on throughout 1971 when she retired.
